The 2024-2025 NBA Season by the Numbers

Highlights of The Top NBA Players and Teams

This graph displays the top 10 players in the 24-25 season by points with a deeper look into their point per game (PPG), field goal percentage (FG%), three-point percentage (3P%), and their minutes played. You can also filter for the top 10 teams and look for the same statistics.

Division Statistics and Team Records

This graph to left displays the inner division record for each team of the 2024-2025 NBA season. You can filter by division using the drop-down button. Each division consists of five teams. On the other hand, the heatmap on the right displays how each team performed against the other teams in its division. Hovering over the graphs will show more details about the stats and records.

NBA Teams Offensive and Defensive Ratings

This graph displays the offensive rating compared to the defensive rating for each team in the 2024-2025 NBA season. The offensive rating is measured by how many points a team generates per 100 possessions. A defensive rating is calculated by the average number of points a team allows per 100 possessions. Hovering over the graph will show what team it represents and their offensive and defensive ratings.

Evaluating Win Percentage Predictions Using Regression

This graph displays the predicted win percentages for each team based on the 2023-2024 NBA season compared to the actual win percentages from the 2024-2025 season. Each point displays the team, their predicted win percentage, and their actual win percentage. The regression line shows how close the predictions for a team were to the actual win percentage.
